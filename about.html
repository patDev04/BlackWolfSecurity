Service

Training

We train all our personnel to assess different situations and threats, staying vigilant and alert they are always on patrol having their k9 partners constantly ready at any moment to deter and stop an intruder.

Our personnel are trained in good manners and interacting with ciilians, being able to de-escalate any potentially hostile encounters that need not end in violence, staying vigilant throughout their shift ensuring the protection of our clilent's assets and wellbeing, trained in dog handling, and accompanied with our world class level trained security dogs. Our personnel are aware of The Gambian lavws in respect to their rights and duties as a security and dog handlers.

Equipment

Black wolf security personnel are all well equipped with combat boots designed for lengthy patrol on harsh terrain, combat trousers, reflective security vest, caps and sweat- shirts. Raincoats and other clothing suitable for the environment and change of weather.

Our staff are equipped with heavy duty flashlights, radios and phones belonging to the company strictly for communicating with the mobile supervisors and emergency response unit. We have a 247 mobile unit to respond as a backup to the guards attending the se- cured site, alerting the police along the way in case of any attempted break-in.

K9

All of our security dogs are of the best working breeds in the world, used by military and police and special forces around the globe. Professionally trained in deterrence and crimi- nal apprehension with a high-level off obedience.

Our dogs are kept at our kennel and headquarters and undergo continuation training 4 days a week along with their on-duty handlers attending training with their Kg counterparts 3 days a week. (See more about our training routines on our website). Dogs are only sent out on duty to guard our clients' per- sons or assets only once having completed their level 2 training and proved to be under the complete control of our security personnel.
